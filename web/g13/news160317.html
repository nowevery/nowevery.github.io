Gigatek Inc. has been certified with Apple MFi License
Gigatek Inc. has been certified with Apple MFi License. Become a part of Apple's accessories supply chain, and give more sure about Gigatek's continuous optimize production efficiency, quality management efforts and achievements.

The Senior Executive Vice President of Gigatek Group-Moses Huang stated: Gigatek is dedicated on the commitment of product quality and service. Besides the specialized production, we provide the complete and reliable solution to our customer. Gigatek would continue uphold the principle of social responsibility and people oriented, compliance with regulatory requirements, fulfill environmental management responsibilities, maintain labor safety and health management, and pursuit of excellence with extraordinary achievements together with customer.

About Apple MFi Certification
MFi is a abbreviation of the original long-form 「made for iPhone」、「made for iPod」and「made for iPad」, there are two types of Licenses: a Development License and a Manufacturing License which is a licensing program and authorization procedure for developers of hardware and software peripherals that work with Apple's iPhone, iPod, and iPad . Apple MFi Program has strict testing standard and operating requirements on where product design, quality and compatibility to ensure the user of audio accessories and electronic accessories that connect to iPod, iPad, iPhone and AirPlay has trusted assurance.
