Our policy & commitment to Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)
CSR is a part of Gigatek Inc.’s overall strategy, which includes healthy corporate governance, a dedication to protecting the environment, conserving energy, and balancing the pursuit of stakeholder interests with our contributions to society.

Our commitment
Strictly follow business ethics and abide by all applicable laws and regulations.
Strictly prohibit gifts, bribes or improper gain (e.g., using a position to obtain improper personal benefits) and conflict of interest transactions.
Ensure that all team members have the duty to report any improper behavior and that any individuals who make such reports are free from any threat or reprisal.
Offer employees a safe and healthy workplace as well as reasonable compensation and certain other benefits.
Extend CSR principles and practices to the supply chain to achieve higher quality performance.
Respect intellectual property rights and abide by confidentiality obligations.
GIGATEK continuously stays abreast of any new topics and trend changes in CSR, and we periodically make adjustments to our CSR programs to cover such changes and implement our CSR goals according to prevailing professional CSR standards.
